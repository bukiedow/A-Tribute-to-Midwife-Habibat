The Wonder that is Victoria Habibat

Midwife Victoria Habibat

The midwife who birthed a nation

Here's a list of some of Victoria's accomplishments and achievements:

  1. 1988 - Gave birth to a son while working with midwives in the west in forms of trainings.

  2. 1989 - Gave birth to a daughter while working with midwives in the west in forms of trainings.

  3. 1991 - Gave birth to a son while working with midwives in the west in forms of trainings.

  4. 2000 - Established the midwives council in the south west.

  5. 2002 - Awarded midwife of the decade.

  6. 2004 - Separated conjoined twins, a first in her community.

  7. 2006 - Received the presidential award for educating local midwives across the country.

  8. 2010 - Received the nobel prize for her work in midwifery across the world.

  9. 2014 - Dies at the age of 70.

"Victoria's life is one that was memorable despite her background and circumstances. It has indeed set the pace for midwifery for generations to come. She will never be forgotten, a woman who indeed birthed nations she is"
---President Barack Obama
